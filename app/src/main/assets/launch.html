Crown Plaza Hotel

NAIROBI

 

Crown Plaza Nairobi is a new and stylish hotel strategically located in the Commercial area of Upper Hill in Nairobi. The hotel is surrounded by Multi National companies, Diplomatic Missions, Banks, Government Offices, Insurers and Hospitals. Our Pillar-less Ball room and 6 bedrooms with state of the Art equipment make it the ideal place to stay and meet.

The Crown Plaza Nairobi is just a 30 minute drive from the International Airport, 10 minute drive to the City Centre and 20 minute drive to the famous Nairobi National Park and Animal Orphange. Crown Plaza Nairobi has 162 superbly appointed spacious rooms which include thirteen suited a Club Floor with its Exclusive Lounge.

All rooms are air conditioned and have high speed wireless internet connectivity. To compliment this is the Business Center, the double storey ultra modern fitness centre and heated swimming pool, Multiple restaurants and a double storey bar, 24 hour room service, a gift shop, money chaging facilities, beauty salon, travel desk, secure and ample parking space, and same day laundry facilities.

Our Hotel staff speaks:
English, Finnish, French, German, Hindi, Punjabi and Swahili.
